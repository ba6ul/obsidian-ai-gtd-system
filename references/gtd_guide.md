# 🛠️ CS Productivity System Guide

Welcome to your optimized productivity system. This system is designed for a **Computer Science Engineer & Content Creator**, blending GTD, Atomic Habits, and scientifically-backed practices.

## 📁 Folder Structure Explained

- **00_INBOX**: The entry point. Capture everything here first (ideas, tasks, random thoughts). Process it during your Daily or Weekly reviews.
- **10_SYSTEM**: The engine room.
    - `Templates`: Pre-built structures for notes and tasks.
    - `DailyNotes`: Your daily hub for habits and tasks.
    - `Reviews`: Weekly and Monthly reflection points.
    - `Guides`: Instructions for your workflows.
- **20_PROJECTS**: Active work with a deadline. Move folders from `Upcoming` to `Current` when starting.
- **30_AREAS**: Long-term responsibilities (Career, CS, Health). These don't have a "done" date.
- **40_RESOURCES**: Your digital garden. Technical concepts, coding tips, and interests.
- **50_PRODUCTION**: The content creation pipeline. 
    - `Ideas` -> `Research` -> `Scripts` -> `Post-Production` -> `Published`.
- **60_ARCHIVE**: Completed projects or resources you no longer need active.

---

## ⚡ Workflows

### 🌅 Daily Workflow (The "Atomic" Start)
1. **Capture**: Throw everything in `00_INBOX`.
2. **Review Inbox**: Move tasks to `20_PROJECTS` or `10_SYSTEM/DailyNotes`.
3. **Daily Highlight**: Choose ONE thing that *must* happen today.
4. **Habit Building**: Use the Atomic Habits section in your daily note to track consistency.
5. **Shut Down**: Review the day, clear the inbox.

### 📅 Weekly Workflow (The GTD Review)
1. **Clear Inbox**: Empty `00_INBOX`.
2. **Review Projects**: Check `Current` projects. Are they on track?
3. **Review "Someday/Maybe"**: Look at `50_PRODUCTION/Ideas` and `60_ARCHIVE`.
4. **Plan Next Week**: Schedule 3-5 main objectives.

### 🎥 Content Creation Workflow
1. **Seed**: Quick capture an idea in `50_PRODUCTION/Ideas`.
2. **Nurture**: Move to `Research` when ready to dig deeper.
3. **Draft**: Use the `Content Script Template` in `Scripts`.
4. **Produce**: Record and move to `Post-Production`.
5. **Publish**: Move to `Published` and create a resource note in `40_RESOURCES` if it's high-value technical content.

---

## 🧠 Note-Taking (Zettelkasten-lite)
- **Fleeting Notes**: In `00_INBOX`.
- **Technical Notes**: Structured in `40_RESOURCES/Coding` using the `Technical Note Template`. Focus on *how it works* and *examples*.
- **Documentation**: Use `30_AREAS/ComputerScience` for long-term learning paths.
